Value Perception vs Dubai

Key Finding

67% of Italian visitors who visited both Abu Dhabi and Dubai find Dubai more value for money overall. However, Abu Dhabi shows competitive performance in F&B (47% vs 53% for Dubai), making it the most competitive category.

Dubai Advantages

  • Transportation - 74% favor Dubai
  • Shopping/Retail - 69% favor Dubai
  • Accommodation - 64% favor Dubai
  • Sites/Attractions - 56% favor Dubai

Abu Dhabi Competitive Areas

  • Food & Beverage - 47% favor Abu Dhabi (most competitive)
  • Sites/Attractions - 44% favor Abu Dhabi
  • Accommodation - 36% favor Abu Dhabi
  • Shopping/Retail - 31% favor Abu Dhabi

Why Dubai Wins Transportation

  • Dubai Metro system (affordable and convenient)
  • Moving around in Dubai is easier
  • Key sites connected by metro/bus
  • More public transportation options

Why Dubai Wins Shopping

  • More discounts/offers/bargain deals
  • Bigger malls
  • More brands under one mall
  • Wide variety of shopping places

Why Dubai Wins Sites/Attractions

  • Sites are closer to each other
  • More offers/deals on attraction tickets
  • Variety of choices for attractions
  • Better experience at attractions

Strategic Insights & Recommendations

Key Opportunity

46% of Italian visitors are same-day visitors, representing a significant conversion opportunity to overnight stays. Same-day visitors spend more per day ($147 vs $140) but shorter duration limits total revenue potential. Shopping emerges as the highest spending category ($49/day), presenting untapped retail conversion opportunities.

Leverage High Shopping Spend

Capitalize on shopping being the highest spending category ($49/day) despite low usage (43%):

  • Create "Shop & Stay" packages combining luxury shopping with overnight accommodation
  • Develop Italian-focused shopping experiences at Yas Mall and Marina Mall
  • Partner with luxury brands for exclusive Italian visitor experiences
  • Create shopping + beach packages (Saadiyat Beach + premium shopping)
  • Leverage high shopping satisfaction (86%) to drive increased participation

Potential Impact: Increasing shopping participation from 43% to 60% could significantly boost per-visitor revenue

Convert Same-day to Overnight Visitors

Develop targeted campaigns to convert the 46% same-day visitors to overnight stays:

  • Create "Stay & Explore" packages combining accommodation with beach, cultural attractions, and shopping
  • Highlight experiences that require more time (Saadiyat Beach + Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque + luxury shopping)
  • Partner with hotels for special Italian market packages with Ferrari World and shopping themes
  • Develop evening cultural and shopping experiences to encourage overnight stays

Potential Impact: Converting 20% of same-day visitors could increase overnight visitors to 65%

Repeat Visitor Strategy

With 82% first-time visitors, focus on converting them to repeat visitors:

  • Implement loyalty programs targeting Italian beach, cultural, and shopping enthusiasts
  • Create "Return Visitor" exclusive experiences at Saadiyat Beach, cultural sites, and premium shopping venues
  • Develop seasonal campaigns targeting previous visitors with new attractions and shopping experiences
  • Leverage the high satisfaction (77%) to encourage return visits

Priority Areas

Address the top visitor challenges: limited tourist info (13%), poor wifi (11%), and transportation issues (10%) to improve overall satisfaction beyond the current 77%.

Information Enhancement

Address the 13% citing limited tourist information:

  • Develop comprehensive Italian-language tourist information centers
  • Create digital information kiosks at key locations
  • Improve signage and wayfinding in Italian at major attractions
  • Develop mobile apps with Italian language support

Digital Infrastructure

Address the 11% citing poor/limited wifi:

  • Expand free wifi coverage at all major tourist areas
  • Improve wifi quality and speed at beaches and cultural sites
  • Create dedicated tourist wifi networks with Italian language portals
  • Partner with hotels to ensure consistent wifi quality

Transportation Enhancement

Address the 10% citing poor public transportation:

  • Improve taxi driver training on key attractions and basic Italian phrases
  • Increase awareness of the Abu Dhabi Tourist Shuttle Bus (only 31% aware)
  • Develop transportation apps with Italian language support
  • Create dedicated transportation packages for Italian visitors

Competitive Challenge

67% find Dubai more value for money overall. Abu Dhabi must leverage its competitive F&B position (47% vs 53% for Dubai) while addressing transportation, shopping, and sites/attractions perceptions where Dubai leads significantly.

Leverage F&B Competitiveness

Capitalize on Abu Dhabi's most competitive category (47% vs 53% for Dubai):

  • Develop "Culinary Capital" positioning campaigns highlighting Abu Dhabi's F&B strengths
  • Create premium food-focused packages combining dining with beach and cultural experiences
  • Highlight unique dining experiences and culinary heritage not available in Dubai
  • Leverage this competitive advantage to drive overall value perception

Address Transportation Perception

Counter Dubai's transportation advantage (74% favor Dubai):

  • Promote the free Abu Dhabi Tourist Shuttle Bus more effectively (only 31% aware)
  • Develop integrated transportation packages for key Italian attractions
  • Create "Easy Access" campaigns highlighting transportation options
  • Partner with ride-sharing services for special rates between beach and cultural attractions

Counter Shopping Perception Gap

Address Dubai's shopping advantage (69% favor Dubai) while leveraging high Italian shopping spend ($49/day):

  • Create exclusive Italian shopping experiences at Yas Mall and Marina Mall
  • Develop luxury shopping + beach packages (Saadiyat Beach + premium retail)
  • Partner with Italian luxury brands for Abu Dhabi-exclusive experiences
  • Highlight unique shopping + cultural combinations not available in Dubai
  • Leverage high shopping satisfaction (86%) to drive increased participation

Leverage Beach & Ferrari Appeal

Capitalize on Italian beach interest and Ferrari connection:

  • Develop beach-themed packages (Saadiyat Beach + Ferrari World combinations)
  • Create partnerships with Italian automotive brands for exclusive experiences
  • Highlight Abu Dhabi's unique beach and motorsport combination
  • Develop beach cultural tours combining relaxation with heritage

Marketing Focus

Leverage strong cultural motivation (18%) and word-of-mouth influence (14%) while targeting mature families (69% travel with spouse/partner, 23% with children, 55% aged 30-49).

Cultural Experience Marketing

Leverage the 18% motivated by cultural experiences:

  • Create "Cultural Discovery" campaigns highlighting Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque (4,802 searches, 77% visited)
  • Develop partnerships with Italian cultural institutions
  • Highlight the blend of traditional and modern culture
  • Create immersive cultural experience packages with beach elements

Amplify Word-of-Mouth

Leverage the 14% motivated by family/friends recommendations:

  • Create "Share Your Experience" campaigns encouraging social sharing
  • Develop referral programs for Italian visitors
  • Partner with Italian travel influencers and beach lifestyle enthusiasts
  • Create user-generated content campaigns around beach and cultural experiences

Target Mature Families

Focus on the mature family market (69% with spouse, 23% with children, 55% aged 30-49):

  • Develop family-focused packages combining beach relaxation and cultural sites
  • Create "Family Heritage" experiences showcasing traditional and modern Abu Dhabi
  • Highlight family-friendly accommodations and restaurants
  • Develop content showcasing multi-generational beach and cultural experiences

Beach-Centric Strategy

Leverage the dominant beach interest (Saadiyat Beach 8,128 searches):

  • Develop Instagram-worthy beach experiences at Saadiyat Beach
  • Create platform-specific content for Italian beach lifestyle preferences
  • Partner with Italian beach and travel influencers
  • Develop hashtag campaigns around #AbuDhabiBeaches and #SaadiyatExperience
